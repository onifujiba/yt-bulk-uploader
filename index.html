I.

Data Collected

In the course of operating this extension, we may collect the following categories of data:

  • Usage data: pages visited, clicks, session duration — solely to improve the experience.
  • User preferences: settings and configurations you save within the extension.
  • Technical data: browser type, extension version, system language.
🔒 We never collect your passwords, banking information, or any content from your private messages.
II.

Use of Data

Collected data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Ensuring the proper functioning and performance of the extension.
  • Remembering your preferences between sessions.
  • Diagnosing errors and improving stability.
  • Suggesting updates and new features suited to your usage.

We do not use your data for any advertising or commercial purpose.

III.

Data Sharing

Your data is never sold to third parties. It may be shared only in the following cases:

  • Technical providers: hosting, error analysis — subject to strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal obligations: when required by law (court order, competent authority).
  • Security: to protect our users in the event of an identified threat.
IV.

Storage & Security

Most of your data is stored locally on your device via the browser's secure storage APIs. When synchronisation occurs, data is transmitted through encrypted connections (TLS 1.3).

Data is retained for as long as necessary for its purpose and automatically deleted upon uninstallation of the extension.

V.

Your Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: know what data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your data.
  • Right to portability: receive your data in a structured format.
  • Right to object: object to certain processing activities.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address listed in the Contact section.

VI.

Cookies & Trackers

This extension installs no advertising tracking cookies. Local storage mechanisms (localStorage, IndexedDB) may be used for your preferences, with no external data collection.

VII.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately.

VIII.

Policy Changes

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. In the event of a significant change, you will be notified via an in-extension notification. The date of the last update is shown at the top of this document.

Continued use of the extension after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
